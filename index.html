Duncan and Jalen's musical adventure!

How to play:

You start with 0 points. Your goal is to complete a melody based on what you hear.

To hear the sample melody, press the "Play Melody" button.

Then, try to compose an ending. When you're ready, press the "Submit" button.

If you want to restart, press the "Restart" button.

Currently, there is only one melody and the submit button always thinks you're wrong. Proof of concept!

Try to guess the ending.
